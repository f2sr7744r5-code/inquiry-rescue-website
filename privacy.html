Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Inquiry Rescue ("we," "us," or "our"), operated by Knostman Studios LLC, respects your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information.

Information We Collect

When you use our contact forms (Inquiry Roadblock Audit request or Free Mini-Audit request), we collect:

  • Your name
  • Your email address
  • Your studio name
  • Your studio's website URL
  • Any additional message you choose to include

If you subscribe to our newsletter, we collect your email address via our newsletter provider, Beehiiv.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your audit request or inquiry
  • To review your studio's publicly available website as part of the audit process
  • To send you the newsletter, if you've subscribed (you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • To improve our services

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes.

What We Don't Collect

We do not request or require access to any private, password-protected, or internal systems (parent portals, student records, billing systems, etc.). Our audits are based solely on publicly accessible information — what a prospective family would see when visiting your website.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services to operate this site:

  • FormSubmit — processes contact form submissions and delivers them to our email
  • Beehiiv — manages our newsletter subscriptions
  • GitHub Pages — hosts this website

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle data in transit.

Data Retention

We retain your contact information for as long as necessary to respond to your request and maintain a record of our business communications. You may request deletion of your information at any time.

Your Rights

You can request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal information at any time by emailing us at hello@inquiryrescue.com.

Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Reach us at hello@inquiryrescue.com.
