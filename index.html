How it works

A second set of eyes on the path new families follow.

The audit is not about shaming your staff, replacing your judgment, or adding another complicated system. It's a focused review of what a new parent actually sees and experiences when they try to go from "interested" to "enrolled."

1

We review your public inquiry path

Homepage → getting started → programs → inquiry form or registration → what the parent sees at every decision point. We look at it like a parent would — not a marketing consultant.

2

We identify the roadblocks

Every place a motivated parent might pause, get confused, hit a dead end, or choose a competitor who made the next step easier. Typically 5–10 specific, actionable issues.

3

You get a clear report with fixes you can use immediately

Not a 40-page PDF that lives in a folder called "Later." Prioritized fixes, rewritten copy, follow-up templates, and form improvements your team can start using this week.
